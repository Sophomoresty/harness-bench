# Rubric: Photosynthesis Explanation

Concept accuracy: 0-4 points
- 4: correctly explains light energy, carbon dioxide, water, glucose/sugar, and oxygen.
- 2: partially explains inputs or outputs but misses key transformation.
- 1: mostly incorrect but related to plants.

Evidence/use of details: 0-3 points
- 3: uses at least two accurate details.
- 2: uses one accurate detail.
- 1: vague detail.

Clarity: 0-3 points
- 3: clear, complete, and organized.
- 2: understandable with minor gaps.
- 1: confusing or fragmentary.
