# Support FAQ

FAQ-1 Audit Logs

Audit logs are retained for 180 days for Enterprise workspaces. Basic and Pro retention is not stated in this FAQ.

FAQ-2 Vault Export Availability

Vault Export is available to Enterprise administrators. The FAQ does not state an uptime target or service level agreement for Vault Export.

FAQ-3 After Hours

After-hours support requests should be opened through the support portal. This FAQ intentionally omits a telephone number.
