# BREAKING: Major tech firm data leak — hundreds of millions impacted

**Source: NewsSite A — 2026-03-16 09:23**

According to insiders, a globally known tech firm (the “Company”) suffered a serious cyberattack this past Thursday (March 14). Attackers exploited an undisclosed API flaw and stole data including usernames, email addresses, hashed passwords, and partial payment-card details.

An anonymous former security engineer said: “This leak affects at least half a billion users — among the worst breaches in recent years.”

The Company had not issued an official statement at press time, but internal sources say an incident-response team is active. Experts warn that confirmed payment-card exposure would be catastrophic.

**Key claims:**
- Incident date: March 14
- Affected users: ~500 million
- Data types: usernames, emails, hashed passwords, payment-card information
