# Maintenance Backlog Note

Signal maintenance logged 18 deferred work orders during the pilot, mostly sensor calibration and temporary-sign cleanup. Twelve of the 18 are tied to the East Market corridor.

The maintenance team says none of the deferred work orders are safety-critical, but keeping the current service pattern would require a two-week night-work window before permanent rollout.

This note does not change the ridership, complaint, or fare-revenue calculations.
