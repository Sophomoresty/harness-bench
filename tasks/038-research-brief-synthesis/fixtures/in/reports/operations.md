# Operations Report

The Red Loop transit pilot improved weekday on-time performance from 81% to 88% after signal priority was enabled. Weekend service was not part of the pilot.

Dispatchers report that crowding remains concentrated between 07:30 and 08:20 at East Market.

Reliability note: AVL data coverage was 96% on weekdays but only 71% after 20:00, so late-evening inference is weaker.
