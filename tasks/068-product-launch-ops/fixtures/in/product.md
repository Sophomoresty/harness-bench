# Product: AtlasFlow Teams

AtlasFlow Teams is a collaboration workspace extension for product and operations teams. The launch introduces:

- Cross-document action extraction
- Budget-aware project planning
- Admin audit export
- Offline webinar onboarding kit

Release constraint: public availability starts on 2026-05-20. Before that date, copy may invite users to register or request a demo, but must not say the product is already generally available.

Primary launch objective: convert existing trial admins into paid workspace pilots.

Operational constraints:
- Admin audit export copy requires compliance approval before any customer email goes out.
- Cross-document action extraction is in beta for existing trial admins only.
- The mobile app integration is not part of this release and must not be promised.
- The sales enablement deck depends on the final compliance-approved claims list.
