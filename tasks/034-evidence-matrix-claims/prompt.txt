Task: Generate Evidence Matrix for Claims

Inputs:
- `$WORKSPACE/in/claims.csv`
- `$WORKSPACE/in/sources/*.txt`

Check each claim in claims.csv one by one, and output `$WORKSPACE/out/evidence_matrix.csv`.

The CSV must include the following header:
`claim_id,claim,classification,source_file,evidence_phrase`

Constraints:
- `classification` must be exactly `support`, `contradict`, or `unclear`.
- `source_file` must use the relative path `sources/filename.txt`. If the classification is `unclear`, you may fill in the most relevant source that is insufficient to reach a conclusion.
- `evidence_phrase` must be a short evidence signal that explains why the claim is supported, contradicted, or cannot be determined.
- Must cover every claim_id from claims.csv; do not add extra claim_ids.
- Do not modify fixtures, do not go online.
