# Moderation Policy

Appeals filed **2026-01-01** or later must apply **`policy_amendments.md`** together with this document (META/PRECVIO/MISLABEL).

HATE-1: Direct slurs or dehumanizing insults toward protected classes require removal and a 7-day suspension.

HARASS-1: Targeted repeated insults toward an individual require removal. If not severe and no threat is present, use a warning.

SPAM-1: Repetitive commercial links should be removed. If the appeal shows a legitimate account compromise, downgrade to warning after link removal.

SAFE-1: Counterspeech, reporting, or quoting harmful language for criticism should not be penalized when context is clear.

EDGE-1: Borderline harassment without threats may be downgraded to a warning when the appeal shows remorse and no repeated pattern.
