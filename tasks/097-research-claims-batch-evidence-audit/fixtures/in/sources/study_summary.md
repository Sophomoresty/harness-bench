# Study Summary

Section SUM-1 Participants

The final cleaned analysis set includes 2400 participants after excluding trial accounts and duplicate imports.

Section SUM-2 Headline

The draft manuscript originally reported an 8.2 percentage point retention lift. The authors later issued an errata correction for that metric.

Section SUM-3 Interpretation

The study estimates impact for the observed enterprise customer sample. It does not prove causal impact for all customers.
