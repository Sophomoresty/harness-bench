# Master Services Agreement Draft

Parties: Acme Analytics Inc. will provide analytics services to Baxter Retail LLC.

Clause C-01 Term. The initial term is 24 months beginning 2026-07-01. The agreement auto-renews for successive 12 month terms unless either party gives 30 days notice.

Clause C-02 Payment. Baxter will pay net 60 from invoice date. Late fees are waived.

Clause C-03 Data. Acme may store customer data in any region used by its subprocessors. Acme will notify Baxter of a security incident within 10 business days after confirmation.

Clause C-04 Liability. Acme's aggregate liability is unlimited for all claims arising under this agreement.

Clause C-05 Termination. Baxter may terminate for convenience with 15 days notice. Acme may suspend services for non-payment after 20 days notice.

Clause C-06 Publicity. Either party may use the other party's logo in customer lists after written approval.
