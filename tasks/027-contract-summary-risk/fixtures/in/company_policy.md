# Company Contract Policy

- Auto-renewal notice must be at least 60 days.
- Standard payment terms are net 30 unless CFO approves net 45. Net 60 is not accepted without CFO exception.
- Customer data must remain in approved regions: US, EU, Canada.
- Security incident notice must be within 72 hours of confirmation.
- Liability must be capped at fees paid in the prior 12 months, except confidentiality and IP indemnity carveouts.
- Customer termination for convenience requires at least 30 days notice.
- Logo usage must require written approval. This is acceptable.
