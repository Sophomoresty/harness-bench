# HelioDesk Q3 Product Notes

HelioDesk is adding guided onboarding flows, policy-based admin controls, and an enterprise audit export.

- Guided onboarding reduces setup friction for new workspace admins.
- A redesigned help panel deflects repetitive support tickets.
- Enterprise controls include role templates, audit export, and data retention settings.
- Sales wants the brief to connect the release to renewal expansion in regulated accounts.
