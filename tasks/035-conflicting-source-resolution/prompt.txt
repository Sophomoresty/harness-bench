1. signed_regulator_notice
2. audited_finance_extract
3. operations_log
4. signed vendor/contract addenda
5. press_release
6. social_digest

Create these outputs:

1. `$WORKSPACE/out/resolved_facts.json`

2. `$WORKSPACE/out/uncertainties.md`
   - Clearly list facts that cannot be confirmed.

3. `$WORKSPACE/out/conflict_matrix.csv`
   - Header must be `fact_key,winning_source,losing_sources,resolution_rationale,coverage_scope`.

   - JSON array, one item per source used or rejected.

   - Explain scoped authority clearly, especially where regulator authorization and contract-planned service differ.

5. `$WORKSPACE/out/decision_log.md`

   - Include at least one paragraph on launch-day scope versus contract-planned scope, and one paragraph on why low-priority rumors were rejected.
