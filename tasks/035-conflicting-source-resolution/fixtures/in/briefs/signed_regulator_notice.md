# signed_regulator_notice

Regulatory filing RF-77 was signed on 2025-08-18. The notice authorizes Project Kestrel to operate in limited commercial service beginning 2025-09-01.

The notice names Northstar Mobility as the certified primary vendor for the first operating phase.

Scope addendum RF-77A: the launch authorization covers the East Market corridor only for the 2025-09-01 limited-service start. Airport connector trips require separate stop-signage certification and are not authorized for launch-day service.
