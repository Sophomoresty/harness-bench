# Travel Reimbursement Policy

- Hotel cap is 300.00 per night unless pre-approved in writing.
- Dinner cap is 75.00 per person.
- Breakfast cap is 25.00 per person.
- Taxi receipts are reimbursable with a valid receipt. Duplicate receipt numbers must be rejected.
- Software purchases require manager approval attached to the packet.
- Missing attachments must be listed and held.
