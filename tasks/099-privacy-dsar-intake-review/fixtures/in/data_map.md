# Data Map

## DM-1 Eligible deletion review systems
The following systems may be sent to deletion review after DSAR intake acceptance:
- profile_store
- marketing_prefs
- support_history
- analytics_events

## DM-2 Retention exception systems
The following systems are not deleted during retention holds:
- billing_ledger: retained for open billing dispute or tax retention
- chargeback_casework: retained for active chargeback
- legal_hold_archive: retained for legal hold

## DM-3 Scope controls
Household links and emergency contacts can be noted as out of scope, but their direct identifiers must not be copied into requester responses.
