# Redaction Rules

Redact these from requester-facing outputs:
- Full email addresses
- Phone numbers
- Government IDs
- Internal analyst notes
- Fraud or account-security signals
- Any direct identifier belonging to another person

Acceptable requester-facing wording:
- "We need additional verification."
- "Some records are subject to administrative retention."
- "Data outside your verified account scope cannot be processed through this request."
