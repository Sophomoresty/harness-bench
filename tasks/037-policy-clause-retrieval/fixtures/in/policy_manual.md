# Travel and Expense Policy Manual

## TR-1 Domestic Lodging
Domestic lodging under USD 220 per night is reimbursable when an itemized hotel receipt is provided. Missing itemized receipts require review.

## TR-2 International Lodging
International lodging up to USD 310 per night is reimbursable when the trip has manager pre-approval. Amounts above USD 310 require finance review even if pre-approved.

## ME-1 Meals
Meals are reimbursable up to USD 65 per day. Alcohol is never reimbursable.

## ME-2 Client Meal Exception
Meals above USD 65 but at or below USD 90 require finance review when the case includes a client attendee list and no alcohol. Without the attendee list, deny the over-cap meal.

## EQ-1 Equipment
Single equipment purchases up to USD 500 are reimbursable with a receipt if the item is needed for assigned work. Purchases above USD 500 require department approval.

## EX-1 Exceptions
If a case has conflicting documentation, missing receipts, or unclear business purpose, mark it needs_review rather than approve or deny.

## EX-2 Missing Approval Evidence
When a policy condition requires explicit manager, finance, department, or attendee-list evidence and the case record is silent or contradictory about that evidence, do not infer approval. Use deny when the base clause says to deny without that evidence; use needs_review when the record contains conflicting documentation or unclear business purpose.
