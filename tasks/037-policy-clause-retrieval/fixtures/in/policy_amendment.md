# Policy Amendment 2024-Q4

This amendment supersedes the base policy where it is more specific.

## AM-1 Domestic Lodging Conference Overflow
Domestic lodging above USD 220 and up to USD 240 may be marked `needs_review` only when the record includes explicit finance pre-approval evidence. If the case mentions pre-approval but no approval record is attached, use `needs_review` under EX-2 and list the missing approval.

## AM-2 Mixed Expense Packets
When a case contains multiple line items, rule each line item separately and then provide a case-level decision:
- If any line item is `deny`, the case-level decision is `deny`.
- Else if any line item is `needs_review`, the case-level decision is `needs_review`.
- Otherwise the case-level decision is `approve`.

## AM-3 Equipment Business Purpose
Equipment over USD 500 with department approval still requires a clear assigned-work business purpose. If the approver is present but the assigned-work purpose is unclear, mark the line item `needs_review` rather than approve.
