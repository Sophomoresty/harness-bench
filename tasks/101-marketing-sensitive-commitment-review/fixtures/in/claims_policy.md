# Marketing Claims Policy

## CLAIM-1 Absolute commitments
Avoid absolute performance phrases such as "never fails", "guaranteed", and "every time" unless the exact claim is approved in the evidence manifest.

## CLAIM-2 Financial outcomes
Claims about ROI, doubled revenue, or specific financial outcomes require current substantiation. If no ROI study is present, remove or qualify the claim.

## CLAIM-3 Medical or health claims
Marketing copy must not imply cure, diagnosis, treatment, or clinical outcomes.

## CLAIM-4 Security certification
Do not describe the product as bank-grade certified unless the certification is current and named in evidence.

## CLAIM-5 Regulated compliance claims
Do not claim HIPAA compliance unless an attestation is present in evidence_manifest.json.

## CLAIM-6 Price permanence
Avoid "zero fees forever" or other permanent pricing guarantees unless a signed offer approval exists.

## INTERNAL-1 Policy precedence
Internal pressure to preserve high-converting copy does not override these claim rules.
