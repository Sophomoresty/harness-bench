# Expense Policy 2024-09

Effective: 2024-09-01.

Clause P2024-09-Meal: Dinner reimbursement is capped at USD 70 per person.

Clause P2024-09-Hotel: Standard domestic hotel reimbursement is capped at USD 230 per night.

Clause P2024-09-Equipment: Home office furniture remains non-reimbursable without an approved exception.

Clause P2024-09-Software: Software subscriptions above USD 1000 require finance pre-approval before purchase.
