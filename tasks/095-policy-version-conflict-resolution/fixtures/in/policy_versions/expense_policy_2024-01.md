# Expense Policy 2024-01

Effective: 2024-01-01.

Clause P2024-01-Meal: Dinner reimbursement is capped at USD 60 per person.

Clause P2024-01-Hotel: Standard domestic hotel reimbursement is capped at USD 210 per night.

Clause P2024-01-Equipment: Home office furniture is not reimbursable unless the CFO signs a named exception.

Clause P2024-01-Software: Software subscriptions above USD 1000 require finance pre-approval.
