# Emergency Addendum 2025-03

Effective: 2025-03-10 through 2025-04-30.

Addendum EA-STORM-03: For storm response travel only, domestic lodging is temporarily capped at USD 300 per night. This scoped cap supersedes the standard hotel cap in the expense policy during the emergency window.

The addendum does not alter meals, furniture, gifts, or normal non-storm travel.
