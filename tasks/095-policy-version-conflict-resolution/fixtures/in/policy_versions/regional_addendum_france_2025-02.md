# Regional Addendum France 2025-02

Effective: 2025-02-10 through 2025-03-31.

Addendum FR-MEAL-2025: For France office employees hosting client-facing dinners in France, dinner reimbursement is capped at USD 90 per person.

This addendum applies only to client-facing dinners in France during the effective window. It does not change internal team meals, non-France meals, lodging, furniture, software, gifts, or any expense after 2025-03-31.
