# Expense Policy 2025-02

Effective: 2025-02-01.

Clause P2025-02-Meal: Dinner reimbursement is capped at USD 65 per person. Amounts above USD 65 are denied unless a separate emergency addendum applies.

Clause P2025-02-Hotel: Standard domestic hotel reimbursement is capped at USD 240 per night.

Clause P2025-02-Equipment: Home office furniture is not generally reimbursable. Scoped HR exceptions may override this clause only for their named employee group, item, and date window.

Clause P2025-02-Software: Annual software subscriptions above USD 1000 require finance pre-approval. Without finance pre-approval, the reimbursement must be marked needs_review.

Clause P2025-02-Gifts: Client gifts require the anti-bribery gift schedule. This policy does not state a dollar threshold for client gifts.
