# HR Exception Memo 2025-01

Effective: 2025-01-15 through 2025-06-30.

Memo HR-ERG-2025: Remote engineering employees may reimburse one approved ergonomic chair up to USD 450. This exception does not apply to sales, marketing, field operations, or contractors.

The memo only changes ergonomic chair treatment. It does not change meal, hotel, gift, or software subscription rules.
